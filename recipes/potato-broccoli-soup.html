Potato Broccoli Soup

Creamy potato and broccoli soup.

Potato broccoli soup is a classic cold-weather soup with a creamy texture from the potatoes. Perfect for a winter evening with the family.

Ingredientes:

  • Broccoli.
  • Potatoes.
  • Cream.
  • Onions.
  • Celery.
  • Butter.
  • Chicken broth.
  • Two different cheeses.

Steps:

  1. Saute onion and celery in butter, add potatoes and broth. Cover and simmer, add broccoli, cover and cook again.
  2. Remove one cup of broccoli and potatoes; set aside.
  3. Using an immersion blender, blend remaining soup in the pot until smooth.
  4. Return everything to pot, stir in both cheeses until melted.
