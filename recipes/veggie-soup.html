Veggie Soup

Delicious vegetable soup for your family.

This is an easy recipe that you can make when you are feeling under the weather, or do not want to spend a long time cooking, while also craving something delicious and warm. This vegetable soup takes 30 minutes to prepare and cook, and you can enjoy it with your whole family.

Ingredients:

  • 3 small potatoes.
  • 3 carrots.
  • 3 small zucchini.
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 3 garlic cloves.
  • 1/4 onion.
  • A head of broccoli.
  • 1 cup of spinach.
  • 1 cup of cabbage.
  • Salt.
  • 1 spoon of cooking oil.
  • 4 cups of water.
  • 1 avocado.

Steps:

  1. First, cut the tomatoes in halves, and throw them in a blender with the onion, three garlic cloves, and a cup of water. Blend until it all mixes.
  2. Peel the potatoes, carrots, and zucchini. Chop into small pieces and put aside.
  3. Chop the head of broccoli into small pieces, place into a strainer and wash under running water.
  4. Chop spinach and cabbage into thin pieces, put in a bowl and soak in water, rinse them, and soak again. Rinse once more time and put aside.
  5. Grab a pot, add cooking oil and let it sit over the fire. Pour the tomato sauce blended into the pot.
  6. When the sauce boils, add potatoes and carrots. Add another cup of water.
  7. After ten minutes, add zucchini.
  8. Let it cook for five minutes, then add the broccoli. Allow another five minutes for it to cook.
  9. Add the spinach and cabbage, then pour another cup of water.
  10. Add salt, and let it cook over a low heat for another five minutes.
  11. Serve with avocado on top. Enjoy!
